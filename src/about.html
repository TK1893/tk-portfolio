About me

Tobias Kraft is a Berlin-based Graduate Engineer of Audiovisual Media and Web Developer. After studying Audiovisual Media (Media Technology) at the Hochschule der Medien in Stuttgart, he worked for over 15 years as an engineer and high-speed camera operator for international film and commercial productions. Driven by his love of technical innovation and the ideal of a networked world, he decided to broaden his professional horizons. After additional academic studies, he has worked as a Full Stack Web Developer since 2024.
