About me

I am a Berlin-based Graduate Engineer of Audiovisual Media and a Full-Stack Web Developer. After studying Audiovisual Media (Media Technology) at the Hochschule der Medien in Stuttgart, I worked in the movie industry as an engineer and high-speed camera operator for international feature films and commercials for over 15 years.

Driven by my love of technical innovation and the ideal of a networked world, I decided to broaden my professional horizons and deepen my programming skills.

After additional academic studies, I began working as a full-stack web developer in 2024.
